What we do

Six specific deliverables. Every one court-ready.

01

Medical Record Review & Chronology

Complete, court-ready timelines of care. Every entry sourced, every gap flagged, every clinical inference explained in language a jury can follow.

02

Case Merit Screening

Fast, honest go or no-go assessments before you commit resources. A clinical read of the record with the clinical questions answered.

Standard-of-Care Analysis

Clinical evaluation against accepted nursing and medical benchmarks. Where the record deviates from the standard, and by how much.

Common questions

The questions attorneys ask first.

Answers that save a phone call. If your question is not here, contact us and we will respond within one business day.

What types of cases do you review?

Medical malpractice, nursing home neglect and abuse, birth injury, personal injury, product liability, wrongful death, elder abuse, and pharmaceutical liability. If the case turns on nursing standards or clinical documentation, we are the right call.

How quickly can I get an initial merit assessment?

Turnaround depends on record volume and complexity. Initial merit screens are typically returned inside two weeks. Rush timelines are available for time-sensitive matters.

Do you work with plaintiff and defense counsel?

Yes. We provide the same standard of clinical analysis regardless of which side retains us. Our job is to read the record accurately, not to advocate.

What deliverables come with a full case review?

Depending on scope: a medical chronology, a standard-of-care analysis, a merit letter with clinical questions answered, expert witness recommendations, and deposition or trial preparation materials.

What are your fees and engagement structure?

We offer flat-fee case merit screens, hourly review engagements, and retainer arrangements for firms with recurring caseload. Contact us for a scoping call.
